About Us

The fast growing pharmaceutical companies in China has great needs for highly experienced experts from US. However, recruiting such talented prefessionals is not easy due to all sorts of reasons. KeystoneAlly was established to answer this market needs.

As a leading pharmaceutical consulting firm that focuses on serving the companies in China and US to deliver high quality professional intelligence, our expert team only includes top-notch professionals with PhD degrees and at least 10 years of proven industry experience in US pharmaceutical companies.

Hiring consultant to work remotely not only streamlines the hiring process but also saves money for the companies. Moreover, by gaining access to a pool of subject matter experts, companies will be in great position to success on their projects.

Our Services

We offer FREE initial consultantion by our chief consultant to analyze the project requirements and to recommend the best fit consultants for the work either on project base or by hourly.
